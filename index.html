How to Use

AI Prompt — Generate Questions

I am a junior clerkship medical student. Please generate [NUMBER] multiple-choice questions on the topic of [TOPIC].

Output strictly only the questions in the format below — no introduction, no commentary, no closing remarks, nothing before the first question or after the last one.

Each question must follow this exact format:

QUESTION: Question stem goes here?
A: First option
B: Second option
C: Third option
D: Fourth option
E: Fifth option
ANSWER: A
TOPIC: [TOPIC]
EXPLANATION: A concise explanation of why the correct answer is right and why the others are wrong.
[END]

Requirements:
- 5 answer choices per question (A–E)
- Clinically realistic scenarios appropriate for a junior clerkship student
- Only one correct answer per question
- The ANSWER line must contain only the letter (e.g., A)
- End every question block with [END] on its own line, including the last question

Replace [NUMBER] with how many questions you want, and [TOPIC] with your subject (e.g., Cardiology, Nephrology). Paste the AI output directly into the paste field above.
